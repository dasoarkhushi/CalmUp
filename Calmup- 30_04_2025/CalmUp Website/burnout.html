Burnout Assessment Quiz

Answer the following questions to evaluate your risk of burnout.

1. How often have you felt emotionally drained or exhausted by your work?

2. How often have you felt a lack of energy and motivation to do your work?

3. How often have you felt disconnected or disengaged from your work and tasks?

4. How often have you felt like you're just going through the motions without enthusiasm or passion?

5. How often have you felt that you are under constant pressure or overwhelmed by your workload?

6. How often have you felt emotionally distant or disconnected from your colleagues or clients?

7. How often have you felt a sense of frustration or irritability toward your work or colleagues?

8. How often have you felt that your work is meaningless or that you’re not making a difference?

9. How often have you found yourself feeling physically exhausted at the end of the day, even if you didn’t do much?

10. How often have you experienced a lack of satisfaction or fulfillment from your achievements?

11. How often have you felt disconnected from your personal values or purpose in your work?

12. How often have you felt a sense of detachment from the people you work with or for?

13. How often have you felt like your work has no positive impact on your personal life or relationships?

14. How often have you felt cynical or negative about your job or your work environment?

15. How often have you felt physically ill or experienced health problems as a result of stress from work?

16. How often have you felt that you don’t have enough time to complete all your tasks and responsibilities?
