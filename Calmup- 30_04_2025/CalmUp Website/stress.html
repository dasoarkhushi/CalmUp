Stress Level Quiz

Answer the following questions to evaluate your current stress levels.

1. How often do you feel overwhelmed by your daily tasks?

2. How do you usually feel when you wake up in the morning?

3. How often do you feel lonely, even when you're around people?

4. Do you find it hard to focus on tasks for a long time?

5. When things go wrong, do you blame yourself?

6. How often do you feel anxious about the future?

7. Do social situations feel draining or energizing for you?

8. How often do you set aside time for self-care or relaxation?

9. How do you handle sudden changes or unexpected problems?

10. Are you generally optimistic about your future?

11. Do you often feel like you're just surviving, not thriving?

12. How often do you compare yourself negatively to others?

13. Do you still enjoy the activities and hobbies you once loved?

14. How often do you feel physically exhausted without obvious reason?

15. Do you have trouble falling or staying asleep at night?

16. How often do you feel tense or on edge for no clear reason?
