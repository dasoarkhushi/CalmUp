Coping Skills Quiz

Answer the following questions to assess your coping strategies and resilience during challenging situations.

1. When faced with stress, how often do you engage in activities that help you relax (e.g., hobbies, exercise)?

2. How often do you talk to someone you trust when you're feeling overwhelmed?

3. How often do you practice deep breathing or relaxation techniques during stressful moments?

4. When problems arise, how often do you break them down into smaller, manageable steps?

5. How often do you remind yourself of your strengths and past successes during hard times?

6. Do you have healthy ways to express your emotions (e.g., journaling, art, talking)?

7. How often do you allow yourself to take a break when you need it?

8. How often do you seek professional help or resources when you're struggling?

9. When you feel negative emotions, how often do you try to reframe your thoughts positively?

10. How often do you engage in physical activities (like walking, dancing, stretching) to cope with stress?

11. How often do you avoid unhealthy coping methods (e.g., overeating, withdrawing, negative self-talk)?

12. When overwhelmed, how often do you prioritize tasks to make them more manageable?

13. How often do you maintain a daily routine to give structure to your day?

14. How often do you set boundaries to protect your mental and emotional energy?

15. When facing setbacks, how often do you remain optimistic about overcoming them?

16. How often do you forgive yourself when things don't go as planned?
