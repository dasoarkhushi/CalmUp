Self-Esteem Assessment Quiz

Answer the following questions to assess your self-esteem and self-worth.

1. How often do you feel confident in your abilities?

2. How comfortable are you accepting compliments from others?

3. How often do you criticize yourself harshly?

4. How much do you believe you deserve happiness and success?

5. How often do you feel proud of yourself?

6. When faced with failure, how do you usually feel?

7. How often do you compare yourself negatively to others?

8. How much do you value your own opinions over others' opinions about you?

9. How often do you stand up for yourself when treated unfairly?

10. How much do you love and accept yourself as you are?

11. How often do you set healthy boundaries with others?

12. How often do you acknowledge your strengths and achievements?

13. When receiving criticism, how do you react?

14. How often do you believe you are worthy of love and respect?

15. How often do you trust your own decisions?

16. How do you generally view yourself?
