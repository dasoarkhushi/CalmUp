PTSD Screening Quiz

Answer the following questions to screen for symptoms related to Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD).

1. Have you experienced repeated, disturbing memories, thoughts, or images of a stressful experience from the past?

2. Have you had repeated, disturbing dreams of a stressful experience from the past?

3. Have you suddenly acted or felt as if a stressful experience were happening again (as if you were reliving it)?

4. Have you felt very upset when something reminded you of a stressful experience from the past?

5. Have you had physical reactions (e.g., heart pounding, trouble breathing, sweating) when reminded of a stressful experience?

6. Have you avoided thinking about or talking about a stressful experience from the past?

7. Have you avoided activities or situations because they reminded you of a stressful experience?

8. Have you lost interest in activities that you used to enjoy?

9. Have you felt distant or cut off from other people?

10. Have you felt emotionally numb or unable to experience loving feelings for others?

11. Have you felt as if your future will somehow be cut short?

12. Have you had trouble falling or staying asleep?

13. Have you felt irritable or had angry outbursts?

14. Have you had difficulty concentrating?

15. Have you been "super alert" or watchful, even when there is no apparent reason?

16. Have you been easily startled by unexpected noises or movements?
