Social Support Awareness Quiz

Answer the following questions to evaluate the strength and quality of your social relationships.

1. How often do you feel you have someone to confide in when facing challenges?

2. When you're feeling down, how often do you reach out to someone for support?

3. How often do you feel truly understood by your friends or family?

4. Do you have people you can rely on during emergencies?

5. How often do you share your successes and good news with others?

6. How often do you offer support to your friends or family when they are in need?

7. Do you feel comfortable asking others for help when needed?

8. How often do you participate in community or group activities?

9. How supported do you feel in your workplace or school environment?

10. How often do you feel isolated or disconnected from others?

11. How often do you celebrate milestones or achievements with others?

12. How comfortable are you being vulnerable with people you trust?

13. How often do you receive encouragement and positive feedback from others?

14. How often do you feel lonely even when you're around people?

15. How confident are you in your ability to build and maintain meaningful relationships?

16. Overall, how satisfied are you with the social support you receive?
