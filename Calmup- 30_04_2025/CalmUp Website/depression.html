Depression Screening Quiz

Answer the following questions to assess your symptoms of depression.

1. How often have you felt down, depressed, or hopeless?

2. How often have you lost interest or pleasure in doing things you once enjoyed?

3. How often have you had trouble falling asleep, staying asleep, or sleeping too much?

4. How often have you felt tired or had little energy?

5. How often have you experienced a poor appetite or overeating?

6. How often have you felt bad about yourself â€” or that you are a failure or have let yourself or your family down?

7. How often have you had trouble concentrating on things, such as reading the newspaper or watching television?

8. How often have you felt physically sluggish or agitated (moving or talking more slowly or restlessness)?

9. How often have you felt that you would be better off dead, or that you wanted to hurt yourself in some way?

10. How often have you felt like a failure or that you have not met your personal or professional expectations?

11. How often have you felt disconnected from others or withdrawn from your social life?

12. How often have you felt helpless or that nothing will ever improve?

13. How often have you found yourself feeling empty or numb, disconnected from your emotions?

14. How often have you felt overwhelmed by daily tasks and responsibilities?

15. How often have you felt a lack of hope for the future or a sense that things will not improve?

16. How often have you felt that you are losing touch with reality or questioning your own thoughts and emotions?
