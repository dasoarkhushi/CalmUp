Anxiety Symptoms Quiz

Answer the following questions to evaluate your current level of anxiety.

1. How often do you feel unable to control your worrying?

2. How often do you wake up feeling anxious about the day ahead?

3. How often do you feel on edge when surrounded by others?

4. Do you find it difficult to concentrate because of racing thoughts?

5. When you make a small mistake, do you overthink it for a long time?

6. How often do you expect something bad to happen, even without a reason?

7. Do you avoid social situations out of fear of embarrassment or judgment?

8. How often do you take time to calm yourself when feeling anxious?

9. When something unexpected happens, do you feel a strong sense of fear?

10. Are you confident about handling future challenges?

11. Do you often feel like you're barely coping with daily life?

12. How often do you compare yourself negatively to others and feel anxious about it?

13. Do you still find pleasure in activities you once enjoyed, or has anxiety taken that away?

14. How often do you experience physical symptoms (like sweating, shaking, or heart racing) without a clear cause?

15. Do you have trouble falling asleep because of excessive worrying?

16. How often do you feel nervous or restless without any clear reason?
