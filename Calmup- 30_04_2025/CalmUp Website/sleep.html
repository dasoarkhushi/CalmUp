Sleep Quality Quiz

Answer the following questions to evaluate the quality of your sleep and identify potential issues.

1. How many hours of sleep do you usually get per night?

2. How often do you have trouble falling asleep?

3. How often do you wake up during the night?

4. How refreshed do you feel when you wake up?

5. How often do you use electronic devices (phone, laptop, TV) right before bed?

6. How consistent is your sleep schedule (same bedtime and wake-up time daily)?

7. How often do you experience racing thoughts when trying to sleep?

8. How often do you feel sleepy during the day?

9. How often do you nap during the day?

10. How often do you consume caffeine (coffee, tea, energy drinks) in the late afternoon or evening?

11. How often do you engage in relaxing activities before bed (e.g., reading, meditation)?

12. How often do you wake up feeling like you had a good night's sleep?

13. How often do you experience nightmares or disturbing dreams?

14. How would you rate the comfort of your sleep environment (bed, pillows, temperature)?

15. How often do you wake up earlier than planned and can't get back to sleep?

16. Overall, how satisfied are you with your sleep quality?
