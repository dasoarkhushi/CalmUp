Work-Life Balance Quiz

Answer the following questions to assess how well you are balancing your work and personal life.

1. How often do you feel stressed because of work?

2. How often do you work beyond your regular work hours?

3. How much time do you spend with family and friends outside of work?

4. How often do you feel like your work interferes with your personal life?

5. How often do you take breaks throughout your workday?

6. How often do you have personal time during the week?

7. How well do you manage work-related stress?

8. How often do you feel that you are unable to switch off from work outside office hours?

9. How satisfied are you with your current work-life balance?

10. How often do you feel physically exhausted because of work?

11. How often do you feel emotionally drained after work?

12. How often do you find yourself bringing work home or thinking about work outside office hours?

13. How much time do you dedicate to activities that help you relax (e.g., hobbies, exercise, etc.)?

14. How often do you feel you are able to take vacation or time off from work?

15. How often do you feel that your work-life balance is improving?

16. How satisfied are you with the balance between your work and personal life?
